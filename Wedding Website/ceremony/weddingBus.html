Wedding Bus!!!



We realise that having a wedding in two different locations is not ideal,

So we have provided some fun transportation to help everyone out!!


The wedding bus shall make two stops before the first venue.

This means that you can make your way to one of these pick-up points and not spend a penny on travel!


  • The first stop will be at Chapel Lane, Hadfield about 2:45
  • The second stop will be at Stalybridge Road, Mottram about 3:00

It will then drop all passengers off at Ryecroft Hall for our Wedding

After the service the bus will then drop everyone off at Phoenix City at 19:00

This means you will only need to arrange from travel home or back to the hotel at the end of the night
